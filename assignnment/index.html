About Us

Our Story

Our journey began with a love for crochet and a desire to share this timeless craft with the world.
What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a business as we discovered the joy of creating
unique and meaningful pieces that bring warmth and happiness to others.


At Justbeaniee, our mission is simple: to create exceptional crochet products that enrich your life.
Whether it's a cozy blanket to snuggle up with, a stylish accessory to complement your outfit, or a whimsical amigurumi toy for a loved one, we pour our hearts into every stitch to deliver creations that delight and inspire.

Each item in our collection is handmade with love and care by skilled artisans who take pride in their craft. We believe in supporting traditional craftsmanship and ensuring that each piece is crafted to the highest standards of quality and durability.
